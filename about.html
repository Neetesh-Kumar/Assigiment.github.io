ABOUT US

Course Help Online has one simple mission: to make it easy for US-based students to access tutoring services online or in person from native English speaking experts.

Our organisation was established to cater to the needs of students who have struggled to adjust to the large-scale nature of today’s educational institutions. Our role at Course Help Online is to ensure that you receive comprehensive and ongoing support throughout your educational journey.

ONLINE TUITION SERVICES

In a matter of minutes, we can connect you with a Course Help Online team member who has specific knowledge and experience in the subject you’re studying. You can ask them for anything you need to help grasp critical concepts – whether it’s assistance with understanding a fundamental theory so that you can apply it in an exam, or guidance with getting a large essay in on time. You can connect via online whiteboard sessions, virtual classrooms, Skype sessions, face-to-face in a library or physical in any other London based location.

There’s no need to feel intimidated, or to feel as though you’re cheating by asking for the support you deserve. All you have to do is fill in our enquiry form, and we’ll respond to you as soon as possible.
